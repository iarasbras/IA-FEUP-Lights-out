Lights Out

Start Menu

Choose your board size and jump right into a puzzle

The goal of the game is simple: Turn off all the lights on the grid.

The Rules

The Toggle: When you click a light, it will switch its state. If it was On, it turns Off. If it was Off, it turns On.

The Cross Pattern: Clicking a light does not just affect that square. It also toggles the four adjacent lights (North, South, East, and West) in a "+" shape.

The Goal: Strategically click the lights until every single square on the board is dark.

Quick Tips

Order does not matter: Clicking Square A then Square B has the same result as clicking Square B then Square A.

Double-clicking is useless: Clicking the same light twice returns the board to its exact previous state. Every light should be clicked either once or not at all.

Strategy: "Chasing the Lights"

If you are stuck, try this common technique:

Start at the top row.

Move to the second row and click the lights that are directly below the "On" lights in the row above.

Repeat this row-by-row until you reach the bottom!
